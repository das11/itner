NZ leadership team

Since our foundation in 1986, our team has grown and diversified, continually reimagining and innovating our offering. 32 years on, our award-winning work has impacted important programmes - both societal and commercial - across the APAC region.

Global leadership team UK leadership team

Our New Zealand business is led by a team who believe in our values to support the great work we do, both for our client base across the APAC region, and for the charitable causes which are so important to us. We're continually looking to work in simpler, smarter ways.
