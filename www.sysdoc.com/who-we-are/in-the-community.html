In the community

A key part of Sysdoc is recognising the importance of giving back to the communities in which we work. Within the team there is a willingness and energy to take action to drive positive change in the world.

Get in touch

Over the years we have provided support across many different areas - including environmental protection, sport, the prevention of child exploitation and disaster recovery.

We have been a silver sponsor of the It's A Penalty campaign for the past three years, providing investment and valuable time and expertise in areas such as social media. Campaigns this year have reached 79.3 million people attending four major sporting events, raising awareness of sexual exploitation of children occurring around these events.

Happy Child
International

Happy Child International is an organisation that champions the rights of the most at risk children and teenagers in the deprived areas of Belo Horizonte and Recife in Brazil and Lubango, Angola.

Aviva Family
Violence Services

Katherine Corich hosted an event in Christchurch, New Zealand to raise money for Aviva Family Violence Services. The event included a Leadership / Process Improvement Workshop.

Global
Angels

We have provided sponsorship toward the bio-sand water filter factory, helping the women to become financially independent and cover the costs of their children’s education. We have also sponsored water wells provided by Global Angels to various locations in India.
